Humble Bundle sells games, ebooks, software, and other digital content. Our mission is to support charity while providing awesome content to customers at great prices

### Humble Partner Program
The Humble Partner program is an affiliate program for anyone to spread their love of Humble Bundle to their audiences and receive a share of the revenue for the Humble Bundle products they are supporting.

There are 3 different products where you can earn money.

- **Humble Bundles**. By default, payments will be divided up with 60% going to the Developers, 10% going to Charities, 15% going to the Humble Tip, and **15% going to the Humble Partner** (percentages may vary between bundles).
- **Humble Choice**. A Humble Partner will receive a referral payment for each brand new subscriber that signs up for Humble Choice through a plan. The payment amount is based upon a percentage of the plan the referred user purchases, **up to a maximum of $11.99**.
- **Humble Store**. The Humble Partner will receive **5% of Humble Store sales** made with their affiliate link.
