NZXT builds and sells premium gaming PCs, custom PCs, software and other PC-related products for the DIY and gaming community.

## NZXT Partner Program
If you're an NZXT fan and you make cool content, they want to work with you!

The NZXT Partner Program gives creators the tools, resources, and support to achieve their goals. This program is currently in beta. 

### What they're looking for
- Content creators. Whether you're on Twitch, YouTube, or TikTok, they want to check out what you make.
- Avid gamers. Console, PC, or handheld—If gaming is your passion, they're looking for you!
- Community focused. They are looking for gamers who share their values and actively work to serve the community.
- Regular schedule. Creators who regularly produce content on a consistent basis are our ideal candidates.
#### Partner Benefits
**Level 1**

- Exclusive merch
- 3 Month membership to SolarStream (StreamerSquare)
- Epic games included. Members are officially supported by the Epic Support-A-Creator Program.
- Partner-only Discord
- NZXT products
**Level 2**

- 6 Month membership to SolarStream (StreamerSquare)
- Featured on NZXT.com
- Exclusive merch
- NZXT build kit
- Sponsorship consideration
