Visuals by Impulse (VBI) is one of the world's largest marketplaces for premade, customizable stream designs. VBI—part of the Elgato and Corsair families—has worked with over 500,000 creators on Twitch, YouTube and Facebook. Their products range from overlays to alerts to sub badges, emotes, transitions and more.

### Affiliate Program
The Visuals by Impulse affiliate program—re-released in 2021—is open to all creators, no matter the size of their audience. The program allows streamers to earn every time one of their viewers purchases a VBI design — using their unique affiliate URL or discount code.

#### Benefits
- Affiliates get a 15% cut of the total transaction price for each successful referral.
- Creators get a personal affiliate URL, which can be customized to their channel.
- They also receive a 5% discount code to share with their audience, which can later be customized.
- You'll unlock access to the VBI Affiliate Area, featuring exclusive analytics and tools to maximize earnings.
- Affiliates receive a dedicated Twitch panel, animated stream overlay, pre-roll video and more.
