Fable Beard Co. was created in 2018 for a simple purpose: to unite adventurers that enjoy the world of fantasy and storytelling with quality beard products that their majestic beards deserve.

### Affiliate Program for Gamers and Cosplayers
Fable Beard Co wants to honor all bearded gamers with a sponsorship opportunity. If you are a gamer that loves to stream your gaming to YouTube, Facebook, or Twitch, fill out their application form with all of your social media links and a reason why you should join Team Fable!

If you are Cosplayer and you have a social media following, Fable Beard Co is interested in sponsoring you as well. Fill out their application form with all of your social media links and a reason why you should join Team Fable.
