Nerdvana Clothing sell premium streamer clothing. Established in 2012, their clothing is inspired by competitive gaming and streamer culture. Designed and printed in Dayton, OH.

### Content Creator Affiliate Program
Nerdvana welcomes streamers and content creators to apply to their affiliate program, which has 3 tiers.

When they sponsor a streamer, they're committed to helping you achieve your dream as a professional entertainer. 

### Earning income as a small to medium sized streamer
Communication with your followers is not only a great way to grow as a streamer, but it directly correlates to making sales and earning money. They reward via commission as handsomely as you will look for every Nerdvana Clothing garment sold from one of your affiliate links.

#### Requirements
Cannot currently be affiliated with another clothing brand or store.
