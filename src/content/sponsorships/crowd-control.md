Crowd Control lets your viewers interact with the games you play on stream. Over 100 games supported, and works on any live-streaming platform. It's a great way to boost your stream income and engage your community in a new, exciting way.

## Crowd Control Partner Program
The Crowd Control Partner Program is an creator program to reward Crowd Control’s most loyal and consistent users. Crowd Control users can apply for partner when they become eligible after achieving certain requirements that measure consistency and engagement using Crowd Control.

### Requirements
- **Engagement**: Must host two monetized Crowd Control sessions per month.
- **Viewer Base**: At least 15 unique monetized viewers required.
- **Revenue**: Generate a minimum of $100 in gross revenue over three months.
The goal of these requirements is to measure your consistency using Crowd Control, your community’s engagement with Crowd Control, and to ensure sustainability profit-wise.

### Partnership Benefits
- **85:15 Revenue Split**: You will make 5% more revenue from Global Coins spent on your channel.
- **Exclusive Access**: Early access to new games and a private Discord channel.
- **Surprise Physical Gift**: Who knows what it'll be?!
For more details on joining and the application process, check out [Crowd Control's Partner Program](https://crowdcontrol.live/officially-announcing-the-crowd-control-partner-program/).

### How to Apply for Partnership
Once you meet the Partner requirements for 3 months in a row, an *Apply* button will appear in the “Become a Partner” page.

That will lead you to a form to fill out as your application. Please note that applying to the Crowd Control Partner Program does not guarantee Crowd Control Partnership.
