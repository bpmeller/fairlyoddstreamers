EwinRacing offers an esports sponsorship program for gamers and streamers. They get a large number of sponsorship requests and aren't always able to provide support to everyone. Every sponsorship request will receive a timely response. 

## EWin Racing Sponsorship Requirements
- 20k followers on one of your social media accounts
- Name
- Location
- Email
- Alias
- Social media profiles
- Why Ewin should sponsor you
Apply here: https://www.ewinracing.com/content/27-sponsorship

If you're afraid you won't be brought on as a sponsor, they also have an affiliate program. 

# EWin Racing Affiliate Program
Becoming an EwinRacing affiliate – means working side by side with a passionate team of gaming industry professionals all over the world, promoting best in class gaming chairs that change the way gamers play the game, cooperating with top eSports teams, and being a part of the video game development community. Gamers from North America to Europe love and value the EwinRacing brand and you can help grow this community.
