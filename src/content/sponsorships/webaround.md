Webaround is a small team of industry veterans who are dedicated to helping grow the content creation side of gaming with affordable and quality content creation tools. They sell green screens, lighting equipment, cameras, and kits that allow you to setup a professional looking stream without spending thousands of dollars. 

### Affiliate Program
Join by filling out an application. Once approved, a member will reach out and get you setup in the system. Get rewarded with a custom code that you can easily track and earn commissions. Individuals who are loyal brand advocates will be able to level up to be a Webaround Gaming Partner. This provides a lot of extra perks and rewards for you and your community.
