The CORSAIR Streamer Program is perfect for anyone ranging from up and coming to well established content creators! If you are competitive, driven to learn and improve, and want to constantly push yourself to new heights, then this is the program for you.

### Sponsorship
Originally Corsair had an in-depth description to all their sponsorship tiers. Now they only have a generic streamer program page encouraging gamers to apply. 

This is not a traditional affiliate program. Corsair is looking to sponsor well-established streamers who can represent their brand.

It's a great company with amazing hardware. We suggest you apply if you're a fan of their products.
