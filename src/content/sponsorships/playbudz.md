Playbudz manufactures and sells grip extenders for console controllers. They're designed to give you better grip, less slip, more feel and comfort.

## Sponsorship
If you're a YouTuber, streamer, or online gamer, and you're looking for a sponsor, fill out their application form. 

Whether you are accepted or not, they will respond to everyone that takes the time to fill out the sponsorship form. 

*Playbudz doesn't list requirements or benefits of their sponsorship program. This is another great opportunity for console gamers.*
