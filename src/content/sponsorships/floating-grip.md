They offer 3 exclusive collaboration programs.

## Affiliate Program
Floating Grip's Affiliate Program empowers you to earn commissions by promoting products through unique referral links on your platforms. Benefits include:

- Personal discount code
- Commissions for each referral

## Sponsorship
Floating Grip's sponsorship initiative offers unique opportunities for your tournaments to feature our innovative products, enhancing the overall gaming experience. While they don't provide paid sponsorships, they're dedicated to delivering meaningful support in line with our mission of transforming gaming setups.

Partnering with the Sponsorship Program equips your tournament with high-quality accessories, including our signature Wall Mounts, LED cables, USB-C cables, and more. These products enhance aesthetics, organization, and functionality, creating an unforgettable gaming environment for participants and attendees.
