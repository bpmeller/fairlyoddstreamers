Mavix was created for gamers looking for a better solution than the traditional race car seat gaming chair. Developed by a partnership combining a team with over twenty years of experience designing ergonomic office chairs and a group of endemic gamers, Mavix has carefully crafted an ergonomic gaming solution that breaks the mold of previous gaming chairs.

## Affiliate and Sponsorship
Mavix has a single webpage for their affiliate and sponsorship programs. Although they don't share any details as to what a sponsorship includes, or what's included in the affiliate program, they do share a list of heavy-hitting streamers who are sponsored by them. Valkyrae, Sykkuno, Swagg, and several others. 

There's no "application process" per se, but they have a webform to fill out for any interested parties.
