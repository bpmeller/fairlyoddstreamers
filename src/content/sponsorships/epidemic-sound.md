Epidemic Sound is a global network of creatives thriving through music, storytelling and collaboration. They provide copyright free music (at a cost) for livestreamers. At the heart of their community is their loyalty program that offers exclusive rewards, subscription discounts and more.

### Epidemic Sound Community
**Start as a Referrer**. When you subscribe to Epidemic Sound, you get exclusive discounts and rewards by sharing your referral link with your friends and audience.

- For every new paying subscriber you bring in, you'll receive a free month on the Personal plan, or a $15 discount on the Commercial plan.
**Level up to Ambassador**. 

- Referrers who bring in over 12 paying subscribers can become Epidemic Sound Ambassadors. 
- You get a free subscription, 50/50 revenue share for every new paying subscriber and paid creative opportunities.
Selected Ambassadors will get the chance to become Brand Ambassadors and take part in globally distributed Epidemic Sound campaigns. Brand Ambassadors get even more exclusive benefits which are tailored to you as a creator and enable you to grow your audience.
