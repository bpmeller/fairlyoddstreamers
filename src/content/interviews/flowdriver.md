### How’d you get your gaming name?
I was trying to come up with a name for my first PlayStation, I was a big fan of Florida back then and my last name is Driver, so Flowdriver was born!

### Why’d you start streaming and what’s your favorite part about it?
I wanted to share my gaming experiences with the community. I had a lot of friends who pushed me to try Twitch and I ended up loving it.My favourite part about streaming is the community that I've built and the stream team I created called StreamNation. The amount of friends I have made along the way is amazing!

### Is streaming a full-time job for you?
Streaming is my full-time job. Before I went full-time 2 years ago, I was working for Xbox testing games while growing my streaming community. When I felt confident enough to do it full time, I went for it!

### Why did you create StreamNation? What’s it like being the founder of a community?
I have a lot of streaming friends, and a lot of my community come to me for advice when they are starting up. So I made StreamNation for my community, to help them build their channel and not have all the requirements that most bigger teams ask of streamers.

### Would you rather have tea and crumpets or an energy drink from X-Gamer to keep your energy levels up while streaming?
I've always been a massive tea girl, so I would pick tea. But if I have had no sleep and I needed a massive energy boost, it would be X-Gamer!

### What’s your favorite console to play on? PC, PS4 or Nintendo Switch?
PS4. Purely because I've grown up playing it and most of my friends still play it!

### How’d you grow such a large following so quickly?
Grinding, literally it is grinding everyday and socializing on Twitter. I also spend most days hanging out in my Discord chatting to everyone in my community.
